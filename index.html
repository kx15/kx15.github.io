01 — Who

Professionally curious.
Incurably so.

Former broadcast journalist. Current communications person by day. The rest of the time: builder of bots and productivity tools, occasional songwriter, and student of how Singapore talks to itself — in four languages and one glorious creole.

The through-line, if you need one: I'm interested in how meaning travels. Sometimes that's a headline. Sometimes it's a hook in Hokkien-inflected Singlish. Sometimes it's a pipeline that turns chaos into something readable. Same instinct, different instruments.

Things that exist now

001

The Bus Bots

Two Telegram bots that know when your bus is actually coming — one for the NUS campus shuttle, one for Singapore's public buses. Built in Python, self-hosted, and quietly saving strangers from staring at bus stops. Small problems, real solutions — my favourite kind of project.

Productivity machines

Nights-and-weekends automations: pipelines that read and format the news, workflow bots that chain tools together, and a small project tracker built on Supabase and Next.js. If a task is boring twice, it gets automated the third time.
